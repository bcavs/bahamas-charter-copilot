## Working Assumptions

### Market

- Bahamas tourism is large enough to support many small experience operators.
- Private charters, fishing trips, snorkeling, scuba, and island tours are meaningful visitor activities.
- Many small operators use informal channels for sales.
- WhatsApp is a common communication channel for captains and guests.

### Customer

- Operators want more bookings more than they want software.
- Operators may be skeptical of AI or automation.
- Operators are more likely to adopt a tool that keeps them in control.
- Reply speed matters because travelers often message multiple providers.
- Clean, professional quoting can improve conversion and trust.

### Product

- Lead conversion is the right first wedge.
- Booking automation should come after quote workflow validation.
- A mobile web companion is a better first build than a native app.
- The first version must feel automated enough to sell, even if it avoids deep integrations.

## Things To Validate

- How many operators publish WhatsApp numbers?
- How quickly do operators respond to inbound inquiries today?
- Do operators currently lose leads because they are slow to reply?
- Are quotes usually custom or standardized?
- What are the most common trip packages?
- What information do captains need before quoting?
- Do captains already use deposits?
- What payment methods are common?
- How often do hotels refer guests to operators?
- Would operators pay monthly, per lead, per booking, or setup plus monthly?

## WhatsApp Constraints

The clean official integration path is WhatsApp Business Platform. That may add onboarding friction for small operators.

Early product implication:

- keep customer communication WhatsApp-centered
- avoid requiring full WhatsApp API onboarding for first demo
- use copy/open-in-WhatsApp workflow first
- later support connected business numbers for operators who are ready

## Competitive Categories To Review

Do not assume this is a blank market. Review these categories before heavy buildout:

- generic tour booking platforms
- fishing charter booking platforms
- WhatsApp CRM tools
- small business CRM tools
- hotel concierge software
- activity marketplace platforms
- AI sales assistant tools

The likely differentiation is narrow vertical workflow plus Bahamas operator relationships.

## Field Research Plan

Build a spreadsheet of 25 to 50 operators with:

- name
- island
- website
- Instagram
- WhatsApp number
- trip types
- visible booking flow
- response channel
- apparent tech sophistication
- notes

Use that list to choose 10 operators for first outreach.
